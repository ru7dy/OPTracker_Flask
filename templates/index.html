OPTracker: Track & Estimate Your OPT

OPTracker aims to provide visualization and estimation for all OPT cases using publicly available information from USCIS. The estimation is based on the average number of cases reviewed in the last ten business days, thus can be inaccurate and should be used as a reference at the user's own risk. Should you have any question or concern, please contact myoptstatus@gmail.com.
